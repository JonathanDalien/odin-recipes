Lasagna

Description:

The recipe is really easy as followed

If u do it correctly it will taste godly

Ingridients:

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Lasagna Plates
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Meat

Steps:

  1. Cock Tomato Sauce with the Meat together
  2. Add Garlic for taste
  3. Cock Milk with baking powder until it is Saucy
  4. Take a Lasagne Plate and add all the ingridients together
  5. Bake it at 200 Grad for 40 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
